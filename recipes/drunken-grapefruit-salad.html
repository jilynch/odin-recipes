Drunken Grapefruit Salad

You won't believe how good this is. Buy the grapefruit in refrigerated jars in your market's produce section.

Ingredients

  • 8 cups refrigerated grapefruit, drained and juice reserved
  • Â¼ cup white sugar
  • 3 fluid ounces gin
  • 8 leaves fresh mint, minced
  • 8 whole mint leaves

Steps

  1. Mix grapefruit, 1/2 cup reserved juice, and sugar in a bowl until sugar dissolves.
  2. Transfer grapefruit mixture to 8 serving cups; top each with about 1 teaspoon gin.
  3. Sprinkle minced mint over each cup. Garnish each cup with 1 mint leaf.
